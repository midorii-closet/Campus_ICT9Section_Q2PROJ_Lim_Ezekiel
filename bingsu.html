Bingsu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup sweet red beans
  • 1/2 cup fresh fruits (e.g., strawberries, mangoes)
  • 1/4 cup mochi pieces
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the shaved ice using a blender or ice shaver and place it in a serving bowl.
  2. Drizzle condensed milk evenly over the shaved ice.
  3. Top the ice with sweet red beans, fresh fruits, and mochi pieces.
  4. Optionally, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top for extra creaminess.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing bingsu!
