Croissant Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Additional butter for laminating (approximately 1 cup)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Gradually add warm milk and mix until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out the dough into a rectangle and spread softened butter over two-thirds of the dough.
  4. Fold the unbuttered third over the center, then fold the remaining third on top (like folding a letter).
  5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes. Repeat the folding and rolling process 3 more times, chilling between each fold.
  6. After the final fold, roll out the dough to a 1/4-inch thickness and cut into triangles.
  7. Roll each triangle tightly from the base to form the croissant shape.
  8. Place on a baking sheet, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  9. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Brush the croissants with beaten egg wash.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm and enjoy your homemade croissants!
