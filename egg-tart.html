Egg Tart Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup sugar (for custard)
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour and sugar. Add cold butter and rub until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Gradually add cold water and mix to form a dough. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into circles to fit tart molds. Press the dough into the molds and trim excess.
  5. In another bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until smooth. Add evaporated milk and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  6. Strain the custard mixture to remove bubbles, then pour into the prepared tart shells.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the custard is set and the tart shells are golden brown.
  8. Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your homemade egg tarts!
