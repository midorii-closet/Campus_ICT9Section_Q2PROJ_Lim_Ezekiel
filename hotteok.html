Hotteok Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar (for filling)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (e.g., walnuts)
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (for filling)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Gradually add warm water and mix until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix brown sugar, chopped nuts, and cinnamon for the filling.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten each one slightly. Place a spoonful of filling in the center and seal the edges.
  5. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add a small amount of vegetable oil.
  6. Place the filled dough balls onto the pan and press them gently with a spatula to flatten.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy your homemade Hotteok!
