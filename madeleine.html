Madeleine Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a madeleine mold with butter and dust with flour.
  2. In a bowl, sift together the flour and baking powder. Set aside.
  3. In another bowl, beat the egg and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the vanilla extract and lemon zest, if using.
  4. Gently fold in the flour mixture, then gradually add the melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared madeleine molds, filling each about 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers spring back when lightly touched.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool in the molds for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  8. Serve dusted with powdered sugar, if desired. Enjoy your homemade madeleines!
