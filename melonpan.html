Melonpan Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups bread flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (for cookie dough)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for cookie dough)
  • 1/4 cup butter (for cookie dough)
  • 1 egg (for cookie dough)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine bread flour, sugar, salt, and dry yeast.
  2. Gradually add warm milk and mix until a dough forms. Knead for 10 minutes.
  3. Add butter and continue kneading until the dough is smooth. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  4. For the cookie dough, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract, then mix in the flour to form a soft dough.
  6. Divide the bread dough into equal portions and shape into balls. Repeat for the cookie dough.
  7. Flatten each cookie dough ball and wrap it over the bread dough ball.
  8. Score the cookie dough gently to create a crisscross pattern.
  9. Let the assembled dough rise for another 30 minutes.
  10. Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
