Mooncake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup lotus seed paste or red bean paste (for filling)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour and powdered sugar. Add butter and mix until crumbly.
  2. Add egg yolk and mix until a dough forms. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each into a ball. Flatten and fill with lotus seed paste or red bean paste.
  4. Shape the dough around the filling and press into a mooncake mold to form a pattern.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the mooncakes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Brush the tops with a thin layer of egg wash.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool before serving.
