Stollen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup dried fruits (raisins, candied citrus peel)
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, sugar, salt, butter, and eggs. Mix until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Fold in dried fruits and chopped almonds. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Shape the dough into a log and place on the prepared baking sheet. Let it rise for another 30 minutes.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Let the stollen cool slightly, then dust generously with powdered sugar.
  9. Slice and serve. Enjoy your festive stollen!
