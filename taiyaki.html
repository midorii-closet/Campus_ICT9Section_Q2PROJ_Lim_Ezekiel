Taiyaki Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup red bean paste (or filling of choice)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the egg, milk, and sugar until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until smooth.
  4. Preheat a taiyaki pan and lightly grease it with oil.
  5. Pour a thin layer of batter into one side of the pan, add a spoonful of red bean paste, and cover with more batter.
  6. Close the pan and cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter and filling.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy your homemade taiyaki!
